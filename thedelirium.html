A few months ago we had the opportunity to introduce you to Geisha with the "Pegasus Tornado" EP. We announced that this work was only a brief introduction to their world of sound. Now we bring you a full album,"The Delirium Introduction", 11 tracks that introduce us to a delirious parallel universe, a magical and organic musical world beyond compare, an album full of melody and harmony, ranging from organic sounds to some of the most psychedelic and experimental electronica being created today. Since the debut album "The Sounds Of Geisha" in 2007, Geisha have shown a profound sense of musical exploration. The new album is evidence of the development of this adventurous and explorational talent. With a combination of electronics, effects, loops, guitars, percussion, strings and perhaps any musical element which may have been available to the creators, this album shows how far it is possible, through sound, to alter the surrounding reality. This is a wonderfully experimental album, yet discreet enough to produce a warm, pleasant environment for the listener to sink into. There is no better way to introduce you to this world than the sound itself ... welcome to The Delirium Introduction.
