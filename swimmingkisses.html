Swimming Kisses was one of the first melodies that Booiamrudolf brought to life. That was around 2009, but now we have two new interpretations of the track. The most recent is the "Almost Ambient Version" created near the end of 2013. It’s the newest remix and expresses the current production style of Booiamrudolf (Be careful, it’s not an ambient track). The "Live Version" represents the way this melody has sounded over the past years at most of Booiamrudolf´s live acts. The last - but not least - tune is the "Original 2013 Version,” remastered and tweaked a little bit in the mix to fit the whims of the producer.

Release Date: 26.02.2014
