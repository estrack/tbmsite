We have chosen Tino Kluge from Black Forest Germany to lead the way of our label. Tino is a real Techno producer, and a trustworthy German Techno agent. However, this EP delivers a special, melodic, kind & sentimental part of his style, making the Katalunia EP a fine example of what we call the closest point between melodic emotional music and Techno. In other words, an emotional approach and a dash of extra spice for the Techno dance floor and the smiley dancers. The remix duty was left in the capable hands of veteran and analogue gear lover from Medellin, Colombia - Omar Raven, who has been around tweaking analogue gear for more than 10 years. This is his first appearance in the international scene. We are excited and proud to announce our very first release on this fresh new record label... Close Records.

Release Date: 10.02.2016
Catalog number: CLO001

All tracks written and produced by Tino Kluge.
