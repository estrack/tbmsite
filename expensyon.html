MrEH A.K.A Eric Houbron is a french Techno producer. His music, full of analog synths and classic beat sequences, is marked by the influence of the 90’s Techno movement. Expensyon EP faithfully represents this style plus his melodic approach. MCMLXXII is a faithful representation of classic melodic techno; a base of analog synths give shape to the melodic structure that accompanies a strong but "slow" beat, a perfect “slow-dance” tune. In the second track Sein-nu a bass arpeggio is the base for a set of subtle melodies that come and go making this track a full synth trip. Finally, Booiamrudolf has created two very special remixes honoring the main theme, Rudolf deliveries two remixes full of deep melodies but strong enough to lift spirits in any big festival or dance floor.
