In a label the name should give a hint to the sort of music we are expected to receive… The Beauty Of Melody eases into new waves of ambient mystery and detailed sound scapes with this gorgeous EP from Irish music act – Geisha. Geisha is an accomplished transformer of sounds, able to really capture a certain essence that we rarely witness in the saturated electronic music world. Inspired by monumental artists such as Brian Eno, Stockhausen, The Orb and Susumu Yokota, it's easy to see where this Pegasus EP gets its level of diversity, originality and complexity… Pegasus Tornado – winds and whistles through ambient domains and leads us into a sort of majestic cinematic state… With its broken beats and swirling eerie ambient washes. Delusions Of Light – full of rich nature sounds – birds, harmonies and a sublime instrumental part towards the end… This track caters for all! The main groove is very driving and big beat with a slight oriental twang. Towards Fulfilling A Prophecy – has a lot to do with what the name suggests… This piece wishes you into an ambient euphoria. Kept in play by the nice simple rhythm and bassline, we keep moving towards a familiar place… Elements of more modern groups such as Bonobo or The Cinematic Orchestra come to mind here. This luminous Pegasus EP is a taste of what is to come on the upcoming Album from Geisha… Truly pure melodic sounds that should not be missed! Tightly rounding up the second release for The Beauty of Melody Recordings and making a heartfelt step forward.

Release Date: 27.11.2015
