We are proud to present our third publication, this time by UK Based artist Lo Five. Ridin’ on Air is the perfect following in our line after Pegasus Tornado and gives a great introduction of what lies ahead in our fourth publication.   This EP contains four themes.  The first, Riding On Air (prelude) is a smooth and quiet introduction that suggests what is to come in the main theme.  A calm melody with soft chords that intertwine with the rest of the track as a quiet but dramatic overture time.  
The second track, By The Gate is an strings and winds tune, number with a nostalgic air and an oriental tone. Ridin' On Air is the main theme of the EP.  A nostalgic chord melody combined with acoustic drum beats makes this theme the perfect combination of nostalgia and hope. Long Day could be a theme for an excellently animated film in the Hayao Miyazaki style.  It could well describe a world of small creatures full of movement, curiosity and colours yet with a disturbing edge.  Listen for yourselves
