Close Records

After some time in the search of beauty within our first imprint The Beauty of Melody, we so the chance to expand our horizons with the creation of a new record label. This time we put the experimentation and eclectic sounds apart, to create space for a particular kind of dance electronic music. A combination of dancey rhythms but at the same time a sense of depth and a sound full of meaning will be the searching on Close Records.
